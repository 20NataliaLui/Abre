Zach serves as the Director of Technology for Hamilton City School District. Before becoming a director, he worked at Oak Hills‘ as an eLearning Coach and Course Developer. The position involved designing online courses as well as assisting teachers in developing an online presence for their classrooms. Prior to the eLearning position, Zach taught American History in Oak Hills and the Houston Independent School District.

Zach is an educator, tech geek, and proud adoptive father of three Colombian kids.

His portfolio, thoughts, and observations can be viewed at Edlightenment: Thoughts on Education & Technology
